How It Works

1. Create a New Thumbnail Template

  • Load your target thumbnail here
  • Customize font, font size, and starting rendering zone to match your vision.
  • Define your own thumbnail components instead of predefined 'heading, subheading, extra'

2. Prepare Thumbnail Metadata

  • Use our user-friendly editor to modify the thumbnail metadata JSON template for each video.
  • Define:
    • Heading Text
    • Subheading
    • Extra Information
    • JSON, Excel, CSV supported

3. Generate Thumbnails

  • Combine your template JSON, metadata JSON, and background images. Generate stunning thumbnails for your videos.
  • Save time and enhance your video content effortlessly.
